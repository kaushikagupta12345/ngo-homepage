About Hope Foundation

Hope Foundation is a registered NGO working across India to support marginalized communities. We believe in transforming lives through education, healthcare, and livelihood programs.

Our Mission

To provide access to basic education, healthcare, and economic opportunities to the underserved.

Our Vision

A world where every child gets education, every woman gets respect, and every family gets dignity.

Our Values

Integrity, Inclusivity, Compassion, Collaboration, and Impact-driven change.

Our Key Programs

Education for All

Running 60+ learning centers across rural and urban slums for underprivileged children.

Mobile Health Camps

Organizing free health checkups, vaccinations, and menstrual hygiene awareness drives.

Women Empowerment

Providing skill training and financial literacy to 3,000+ women every year.

Environmental Care

Plantation drives, waste management workshops and clean-up events across cities.

Success Stories

Rani’s Journey from Slum to School

Rani, a 10-year-old girl from Delhi’s slum, was enrolled in our free education center. Today, she reads fluently and dreams of becoming a teacher.

Sunita: A Stitch in Time

Through our stitching program, Sunita now earns Rs. 8,000/month and supports her family independently.

Clean Water, Healthier Lives – Village Sundarpur

The villagers of Sundarpur relied on a polluted pond for drinking water. Through our Clean Water Initiative, we installed hand pumps and filtration units. Today, waterborne diseases have dropped by 90%.

Meena's Fight Against Child Marriage

At just 14, Meena was about to be married off. Our field workers intervened and counseled her parents. Today, Meena is pursuing a diploma in social work and actively campaigns against child marriage in her village.
